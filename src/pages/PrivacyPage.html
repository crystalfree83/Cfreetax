Security & Privacy Policy

At C. Free Virtual Tax Services, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal and financial information. This policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect

We collect information necessary for the preparation of your tax returns and for providing our tax-related services. This may include:

  • Personal identification information (name, address, Social Security Number, date of birth)
  • Financial information (income, deductions, credits, bank account details)
  • Contact information (phone number, email address)
  • Information about your dependents

We collect this information directly from you through forms, interviews, and secure document uploads.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the purpose of:

  • Preparing and filing your tax returns
  • Providing tax advice and consulting services
  • Communicating with you regarding your tax matters
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements

Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. These include:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted through our secure client portal is encrypted.
  • Secure Storage: Your digital documents are stored on secure, encrypted servers with restricted access.
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel have access to your information.
  • Regular Updates: Our systems and software are regularly updated to protect against vulnerabilities.
  • Physical Safeguards: For any physical documents, we employ secure storage practices.

Disclosure of Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. Your information will only be disclosed in the following circumstances:

  • With your express consent.
  • As required by law (e.g., to the IRS or other government agencies).
  • To third-party service providers who assist us in operating our business and serving you (e.g., secure client portals, e-filing software), provided they agree to keep this information confidential and secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal and retention requirements.

To exercise these rights, please contact Crystal Free directly.

Retention of Information

We retain your tax records and related information for as long as necessary to comply with legal obligations (e.g., IRS record retention requirements), resolve disputes, and provide ongoing services to you.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Security & Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "last updated" date will be revised. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
