Frequently Asked Questions

What is an Enrolled Agent (EA)?

An Enrolled Agent (EA) is a tax professional authorized by the U.S. Department of the Treasury to represent taxpayers before the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). EAs are required to pass a comprehensive examination covering all aspects of taxation and adhere to ethical standards. They are specialists in taxation and can represent any taxpayer regarding audits, collections, and appeals.

How does virtual tax preparation work?

Virtual tax preparation means you can get your taxes done from anywhere! We use secure online portals and communication methods (email, phone, video calls) to collect your documents, discuss your situation, and prepare your return. This saves you time and provides flexibility.

What documents will I need to provide?

Common documents include W-2s, 1099s (for interest, dividends, independent contractor income), mortgage interest statements (1098), property tax statements, charitable contribution records, and information related to any dependents. We will provide you with a detailed checklist based on your specific situation.

Is my information secure with C. Free Virtual Tax Services?

Absolutely. We prioritize the security and privacy of your sensitive financial information. We use encrypted platforms for document exchange and adhere to strict data protection protocols. Please refer to our Security & Privacy Policy for more details.

How much do your services cost?

Our fees vary depending on the complexity of your tax situation. We offer competitive and transparent pricing. After an initial consultation (often free), we can provide you with an estimate. Please contact us for a personalized quote.

Can you help with past due taxes or amended returns?

Yes, Crystal Free is experienced in assisting clients with prior year tax filings, amended returns, and responding to IRS notices. Don't hesitate to reach out if you have unfiled returns or received correspondence from the IRS.
